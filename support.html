Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes. All speech recognition and translation happen entirely on your iPhone. Your audio, transcripts, and translations never leave your device. Talyno has no backend, no account, and no analytics SDK.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Talyno works fully offline from first launch. The only network activity is an optional speech-model download you can trigger manually in Settings → Transcription Model. Skipping the download is fine — the app works with the bundled model.

Which languages are supported?

English, Traditional Chinese (zh-Hant), Japanese, Korean, and German. On-device translation packs are downloaded automatically the first time a language pair is used.

How do I improve transcription accuracy?

Go to Settings → Transcription Model and download the Accurate model (~216 MB). It runs on the Neural Engine and improves accuracy significantly over the Basic bundled model.

What permissions does Talyno need?

Only the microphone. No contacts, location, camera, or other permissions are requested.

Is Talyno free?

Yes. Talyno is free with no in-app purchases and no subscription.
